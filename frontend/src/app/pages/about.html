About CarRental

Our Story

Founded in 2019, CarRental began with a simple mission: to provide Kenyans with access to premium vehicles for their travel needs. What started as a small fleet of 5 vehicles has grown into one of Kenya's leading car rental services.

We understand that every journey is unique, whether it's a business trip, family vacation, or special occasion. That's why we've curated a diverse fleet of well-maintained vehicles to meet every need and budget.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and safety has made us the preferred choice for thousands of customers across Kenya.
